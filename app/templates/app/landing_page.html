Swahilipot Hub Foundation

Case Management Operations

The Swahilipot Hub Foundation, based in Mombasa, Kenya, empowers youth aged 18-35 through innovative case management. We connect "opportunity youth" to technology, creative arts, and heritage opportunities, bridging the gap between talent and sustainable livelihoods.

How We Operate

Personalized Mentorship

Led by experts like Chief Mentor Mahmoud Noor, our case managers co-design mentorship programs with youth. With 150 mentors (targeting 3,000 in five years), we support 3,000 mentees (aiming for 20,000), offering tailored guidance to unlock their potential.

Technology-Driven Matching

Partnering with DataKind, we use a machine learning algorithm to efficiently match youth with jobs, training, and mentors. This scalable tool ensures fair, data-driven outcomes with minimal manual effort.

Our Process

1

Outreach

Engage youth through Youth Hub Networks across Mombasa’s six sub-counties.

2

Data Collection

Profile skills, interests, and goals to create actionable plans.

3

Matching & Support

Use tech tools and mentor networks to connect youth to opportunities.

Are You a Target Applicant?

If you’re a youth aged 18-35 in Mombasa seeking mentorship, skills development, or career opportunities in tech or creative fields, we’re here for you!
